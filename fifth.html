Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie receipe has been in my family for generations and never failes to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium sized apples,peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375oF.
  2. In a bowl,mix the suger and cinnamon
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
